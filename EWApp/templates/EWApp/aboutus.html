What We do at TotalRefurb?

  • Visual Inspection Of Waste Product
  • Repair Attempt and replacement of Parts
  • Deep Cleaning of Product
  • In-Depth Testing
  • If tests fail we recover electronic components from the PCBs of the product
  • If tests pass we sell it for cheap prices on our website

Why TotalRefurb?

TotalRefurb is not only directed towards customers who want to buy/sell old products, but also aims to reinforce the idea of easy repairibility and reliable part outsourcing. The concept of Right to Repair is gaining a lot of momentum across the world. We aim to do our part in contributing to it
